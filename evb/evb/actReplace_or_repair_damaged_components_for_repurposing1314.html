Activity: Replace or Repair Damaged Components for Repurposing

Description

Notes:

Definitions:
Repair - returning a faulty, worn or broken product or component back to a usable state. From ISO 8887-2:2023.

Replace - remove a part or component and mount a new or checked part or component with the same characteristics. Adapted from ISO 9879:2024.
