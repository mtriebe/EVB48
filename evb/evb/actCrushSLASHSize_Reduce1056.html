Notes:

Definitions:
Crushing - mechanical reduction of the particle size of a material by fracturing large pieces into multiple smaller pieces. From ISO 14284:2022.

Particle size reduction - process of crushing or grinding the sample to reduce the particle size. From ISO 1213-2:2024.
