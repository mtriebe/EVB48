Activity: Test Thermal Behavior

Description

Notes:

Definition:
Thermal test - evaluates a battery's performance and safety under various temperature conditions, including extreme heat, cold, or rapid temperature changes, to ensure its reliability, longevity, and suitability for its intended use. Adapted from IEC 62133, UL 2054, and SAE J2464.

Thermal runaway - uncontrollable condition whereby a cell or a battery overheats and reaches very high temperatures in very short periods (seconds) through internal heat generation caused due to an internal short or due to an abusive condition. From ISO 17546:2024.
